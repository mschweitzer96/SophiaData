About Us

We empower businesses and researchers to build their individual world formulas! There has never been more data publicly available. We believe that mining and analyzing publicly available, unstructured data and connecting it to existing datasets can generate enormous value. In order to utilize the world's data, we have built Sophia. An Artifical Intelligence that satisfies your needs with real-time and customizable datasets. We call those datasets "connectors".

Just try it out and define what kind of dataset you are looking for and Sophia will automatically start building your individual connector to the outside world.

If you don't know what you are looking for yet, just browse our existing connectors and previous projects.
